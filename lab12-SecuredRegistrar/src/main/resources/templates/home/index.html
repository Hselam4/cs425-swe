Maharishi University of Management offers admissions to diverse Bachelors, Masters, and Ph.D. programs for its applicants coming from different corners of the world. It is now popularly called as Maharishi International University. The university boasts an acceptance rate of 100% with an application calendar for the fall semester. International applicants applying to UG courses at Maharishi International University are required to have a GPA of 2.5 (76-79%) and those applying to PG courses need 3.0 (83-86%). Applications are invited for fall and spring semesters from international applicants.
